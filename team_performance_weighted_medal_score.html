Team Performance Trends by Weighted Medal Score

This visualisation is designed to answer how teams’ performances develop over time and whether detectable trends exist. The horizontal axis shows year, while the vertical axis shows team performance measured by weighted medal score (3 × Gold + 2 × Silver + 1 × Bronze). Each line represents a national team.
