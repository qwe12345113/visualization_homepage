Participation Size and Medal Efficiency

This visualisation addresses how team performance is associated with participation size and medal efficiency over time. Each point represents a country in a given year. The horizontal axis shows participation size (total participants), while the vertical axis shows medal efficiency or weighted efficiency. Point size encodes total medal count. Use the controls to switch between Olympics and Paralympics, choose Summer or Winter, and move through time with the year slider.
